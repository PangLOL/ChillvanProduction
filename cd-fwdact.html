Client

Chillvan Production x FWD

We are honoured to be invited for a collaboration by FWD ACT Team. Here we were given a task to engage the brilliant image of Kevin, the star of this work, working with the ACT Team in FWD. A wide degree of creative freedom was given as we could try out different skills we harness over the years.

Concept

If we have to make it, make it the best of the best.

Never repeats what others did, not even yourself. For long enough, most of the talents promotional videos for any insurance company always falls into the same monotony. This makes it difficult to entertain viewers. We are lucky to be entrusted by FWD ACT Team, our director could then provide a strikingly new perspective, which excels at both the looks, and the vibe, to really sales the piece.
