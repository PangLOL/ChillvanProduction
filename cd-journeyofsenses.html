Client

超凡映業 x 尚品娛樂
Chillvan Production x SONG Entertainment

We were requested to produce a music video with the new release, Journey Of Senses, by the Artist Elise Suen.

Concept

Senses can take us to place where we can’t physically go.

This is a timey piece, as it was produced during the pandemic lock down in most of the world. Everyone was quite literally grounded to there own cities. No vacations, no flying to other countries. We were trying our best to visualise the message from the lyrics, while also avoid mentioning the pandemic situations. We bring the views to a trip driven by senses, we have no destination, no time limit, just wandering in the vast world of senses. This is also a hasty production, we managed.
