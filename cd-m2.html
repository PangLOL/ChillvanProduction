Director's Statement

超凡映業 x 梁國鵬
Chillvan Production x Leung Kwok Pang

I love my secondary school, and I hate to see the things I love slowly dies down. This is a work that’s truly comes from my heart, I cherish every bit of this production. And I count on this work to remind the new students that came into my secondary school, it’s easy to loss the things you love, please don’t let them go.

Concept

「終須失去何又怕？怕在未曾跌倒過。」
  “Don’t be scared of loosing, in case you’ve never fell before.”

When facing the pressure from a more superior power, what would you choose?
「每一步向前，每一份信念，未曾慢一拍，推進吧！」 “Every step ahead, every thoughts as one, never slows down, pushing on.”
In this school, we students chose to fight, we chose to speak out. This is a war between the students, and the school. 《住在M2走廊的人》- People Who Live In M2 is a project that involved the whole crew of Chilean Production, we wielded all our might for this passion project. This is a self-funded music film, besides based on a series of real event, several original songs were produced to support the whole story telling.
「坐以待斃？定係願意就位，為大家甘願自毁？」“Sitting still waiting, or getting ready, to die for everyone else.”
This is also our director’s love letter to his secondary school live, and also a reminder for the new students of the school.
「每日過渡虛耗，得過且過。」“Let the days pass by, muddle along.”
We hope after watching this piece, viewers can understand the vibe of this school, and for the new students to understand the haste that actions have to be made immediately.
