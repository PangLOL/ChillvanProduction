Client

超凡映業 x 文化者
Chillvan Production x The Culturist

We were requested to provide a secure online live streaming service for Arts Go Digital x The Culturist 劇場扭計骰 Artists Talk Event.

Concept

Enjoy the seminar while sitting soundly at home.

Arts Go Digital Platform Scheme 藝術數碼平台計劃, was presented by Hong Kong Arts Development Council and funded by The Hong Kong Jockey Club Charities Trust. There were 68 funded projects cover a wide range of art forms and disciplines, presenting creation in an innovative form online & offline. This time, Arts Go Digital teamed up with The Culturist and hosted a talk event for the artists involved to provide the insight of their artwork for the viewers. As the global pandemic still persist, many event had to go online. We provide the online live streaming solution to the whole event, making sure the connection and the visual are secure and decent, while maintaining the talk show with guest from online.
This live production is especially different, besides the 5 guests present on location, there was also a guest situated in Germany. We provided a online meeting conference solution which we connected the foreign guest with Zoom. This allowed him to join our talk seamlessly with minimal delay, so all guests can share their works online. Apart from that, for ensuring the ease of viewing, we also implemented lower-third titles for each guest so that viewers can distinguish all the guests clearly.
