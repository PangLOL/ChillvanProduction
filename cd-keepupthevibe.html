We were given unlimited creative freedom and every one of our team members were thrilled in this particular production. This gave birth to this piece of work,《球魂不滅》- Keep Up The Vibe.

Concept

Never stop the trying, innovating, and breaking through is the momento of Chillvan Production.

《球魂不滅》- Keep Up The Vibe is no exception. We were given the task to assist the up and coming director, Captain Chan (former staff in The Culturist), to promote the book - 球魂不滅: 美國籃球眾星誌 by Roy Cheng, with a 2-part short film.
The story speaks about our main protagonist, living in a mundane life, rediscovering his left out draft drawings for his best friends. This reminded his glorious days of playing basketball with his best friends, also the promise he made for him to finish all the oil paintings of NBA starts for his friends. He picked up his paint brushes again, determined. He decided he would start all over and finally fulfil the promise he made long ago, in order to spark up the fire for basketball within them, and also the priceless friendship they used to hold.
