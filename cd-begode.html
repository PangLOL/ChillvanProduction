Client

超凡映業 x 深圳市比高德信息科技有限公司
Chillvan Production x Shenzhen Bigdatas Information Technology Ltd

We got invited to a collaboration with Bigdatas Information Technology Ltd for promoting their brand new technology in their electric unicycle.

Concept

It's dangerous, that makes it even better.

We are requested to showcase a new technology in the electric unicycle industry. This is a new for of unicycle which features a substantial suspension system. Normally, riders aim for the thrill of the ride of the electric unicycle, yet this new-born contradict quite a bit in providing also comfort in the ride. These made our director took some extra steps in visualising such contradicted settings. With the help of a drone camera, and action camera, we created stable visuals in rugged environment with a great sense of speed. This is all done for the extraordinary viewing experience for the viewers.
