Client

超凡映業 x 尚品娛樂
Chillvan Production x SONG Entertainment

We were requested to produce a music video with the new release, Keyman, by the Artist Simon On.

Concept

You are what you can rely on, and only you can make a difference for yourself.

A set idea draft and plan was given in advance of the pre-production phase, we work with SONG Entertainment to improve the base idea and worked on a larger world building and story-telling. We aim to visualise the message that the lyrics gives us, and tell the story of a man losses himself on blindly complying to what his audiences requested. Sadly he came to his demise as he conducted some dangerous act for the views. His spirit realises how stupid it was for him to dedicated his whole life to the views, and broke the dimension to save himself from dying. It was quite challenging for such a short production time, yet we managed.
